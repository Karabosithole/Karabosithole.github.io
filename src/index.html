About Me

Hey there! I’m Karabo Sithole, an aspiring software engineer based in Johannesburg. My passion lies in exploring the vast world of technology, constantly learning, and pushing my boundaries. With a solid foundation in Java, Python, and JavaScript, and experience working with tools like Git, Docker, and SQL, I’m eager to expand my knowledge and tackle new challenges across various tech domains.

What excites me most is the opportunity to grow through hands-on project work, collaboration, and continuous learning. I thrive in environments where I can explore different languages, tools, and methodologies, and I'm always looking for ways to apply what I learn to real-world problems. Whether it's building backend systems, designing user-friendly frontends, or diving into cloud computing, I’m committed to becoming a well-rounded developer who delivers high-quality solutions.

At my core, I believe in adaptability and learning through experience. I'm excited to embrace opportunities that broaden my technical expertise and prepare me for a successful career in software development. With a strong work ethic, a love for both innovation and fun, and a passion for self-improvement, I’m ready to contribute to projects that make an impact.
